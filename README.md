# 📚 Paper2Prod ML Systems Research Papers & Implementation (2026)

A curated, structured research reading list for **Senior / Lead Data Scientist or Senior AI Engineer roles** at top product companies (FAANG+, unicorns, elite AI labs).

---

## 🚀 Why This Exists

At Senior level, you're not just expected to know models — you're expected to:

- Understand **why systems work**
- Translate research into **production systems**
- Make **trade-offs (latency, scale, cost, accuracy)**
- Connect **ML → product impact → business metrics**

This repository curates **foundational + state-of-the-art (2025–2026 relevant)** research papers across all critical domains.

# 🧠 12-Week Study Plan

### Weeks 1–4 → Foundations
- Classical ML
- Deep Learning basics
- Bias-variance, XGBoost, Transformers

### Weeks 5–8 → Systems
- Recommender systems
- NLP (BERT, GPT, RAG)
- MLOps + A/B testing

### Weeks 9–10 → Advanced
- RL, GNNs, Diffusion models
- Data-centric AI

### Weeks 11–12 → Leadership Layer
- Responsible AI
- System design thinking
- Trade-offs + product impact
